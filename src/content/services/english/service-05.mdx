Ransomware attacks on churches increased 300%+ from 2021 to 2024 (CISA). Church Data Protection helps you secure donor financial information, member records, and children's program data — so your congregation's trust is never compromised.

Your church holds sensitive data — financial records, personal information, children's program details. Protecting that trust is not just a technical task; it is a form of stewardship. Most mid-sized churches have no documented security policy, no backup plan, and no one monitoring for threats. Church Data Protection gives you a clear, prioritized plan to secure what matters most.

## What You Get [.h2 .font-bold .font-secondary]

- Security assessment that identifies vulnerabilities in your current systems
- Prioritized action plan based on risk level and budget
- Policy templates for data handling, access control, and incident response
- Backup and recovery planning so your ministry continues even after an incident
- Ongoing monitoring recommendations to keep your church's data secure

## Why This Matters

A single ransomware attack can take down your giving platform, member database, and communication tools for weeks. Churches are targeted because they typically have weaker security than businesses of similar size — and attackers know it. Church Data Protection frames security as faithful stewardship of what God has entrusted to your church — not fear, but responsibility.
