Churches overpay MSPs by 40–60% above market rate because no one is advocating for you. Church Vendor Selection & Review puts a knowledgeable partner in your corner — evaluating vendors, reviewing contracts, and ensuring you pay for technology that fits your church, not technology that fits a sales quota.

Vendor selection is one of the highest-impact decisions your church makes about technology. Yet most churches evaluate vendors based on the pitch that sounds best, not the contract terms that protect them. Without objective criteria and someone reviewing the fine print, churches consistently overpay and under-serve their staff.

## What You Get [.h2 .font-bold .font-secondary]

- Objective vendor evaluation based on your church's specific needs and budget
- Contract review that identifies overcharges, vague SLAs, and lock-in clauses
- Negotiation support so you get fair terms from every vendor
- Ongoing vendor performance review to ensure you continue getting value
- A clear framework for future vendor decisions your staff can use independently

## Why This Matters

The average mid-sized church works with 5–8 technology vendors. Without someone reviewing contracts and evaluating performance, costs accumulate, tools overlap, and the church pays for capabilities it never uses. Church Vendor Selection & Review gives you an advocate who understands both the technology and the vendor landscape — so you invest in ministry, not overpriced services.
