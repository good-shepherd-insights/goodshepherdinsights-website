The average church runs **5–8 disconnected software tools**—a ChMS, a giving platform, an accounting system, an email platform, a check-in system, a website, and a communication tool. Most of these were not selected as part of a unified strategy, and none were integrated at implementation. Consequently, they are not telling the same story about the same people.

The result is an operational tax paid every single week: staff re-enter the same data into multiple systems, giving records in the platform don't match the ChMS, and attendance data lives in a check-in silo that doesn't communicate with your engagement tools. Reports are assembled manually in spreadsheets—slowly, inconsistently, and inaccurately.

Leadership is forced to make critical decisions about staffing, ministry investment, capital campaigns, and outreach on data they simply cannot trust.

Good Shepherd Insights builds the single source of truth. As your independent technology executive, we audit your tech stack, map every integration point, build or configure every connection, clean the data, and establish the governance protocols that keep it clean. We don't sell you another platform; we build the architecture and governance that make the platforms you already own work together.

## The High Cost of Integration Debt [.h2 .font-bold .font-secondary]

For many churches, the pain of disconnected data isn't theoretical—it's a weekly crisis. 

Imagine the finance director pulling the end-of-year giving report. The number in the giving platform is $847,000. The number in QuickBooks is $831,000. The number in the ChMS is $819,000. Nobody can explain the discrepancy. The year-end contribution statements go out late because reconciling three systems manually takes two weeks of staff time.

Meanwhile, the Executive Pastor wants to know how many first-time guests from January are still attending in June. The answer is unattainable because attendance is in the check-in system, guest records are in the ChMS, and follow-up is in a separate email tool. Nobody ever connected them.

This is the "integration debt" that costs your church money, staff time, and decision-making clarity. GSI resolves this debt and keeps it closed.

## The Data Integration & Management Framework [.h2 .font-bold .font-secondary]

We don't apply generic IT fixes. We implement a church-exclusive integration architecture designed for ministry operations.

### Tech Stack Audit & Integration Mapping
Before building a single connection, we perform a structured review of every software tool your church is running. We catalog every SaaS subscription—including "shadow IT" tools staff use without oversight—and create a current-state diagram of how data actually moves (or fails to move) between systems.

**What we deliver:**
- **Full Software Inventory:** A complete catalog of all digital tools and subscriptions.
- **Data Flow Mapping:** A visual map of where data enters, exits, and stalls in your ecosystem.
- **Redundancy & Gap Identification:** Pinpointing overlapping tools and missed integration opportunities.
- **Target-State Architecture:** A documented blueprint showing every integration point, data direction, and sync frequency.

### Giving Platform Integration & Optimization
Online giving is the fastest-growing channel for church donations, with properly configured systems seeing an average **32–40% increase in donations**. However, without integration, you simply gain a revenue channel and an operational nightmare simultaneously.

We connect platforms like Pushpay, Tithely, Vanco, and Givelify directly to your ChMS and accounting systems (QuickBooks, Aplos, Xero).

**What we deliver:**
- **ChMS ↔ Giving Sync:** Donor record matching, transaction sync, and household giving attribution.
- **Accounting Integration:** Automated batch deposit reconciliation and fund-to-account mapping.
- **Recurring Giving Optimization:** Configuration of ACH/card retry logic and lapsed donor workflows.
- **Automated Giving Journeys:** First-time giver thank-yous and capital campaign pledge reminders.
- **Year-End Workflow:** A streamlined process to generate contribution statements in hours, not weeks.

### Reporting & Dashboard Build
Most churches have plenty of data, but no intelligence. We replace manual spreadsheet assembly with automated, reliable, and actionable ministry intelligence.

**What we deliver:**
- **Ministry KPI Definition:** Defining the metrics that actually matter (e.g., weekly attendance trends, giving per household, and guest retention).
- **Automated Report Scheduling:** Weekly and monthly summaries delivered directly to leadership inboxes.
- **The Leadership Dashboard:** A real-time view of the 5–7 metrics that tell the story of ministry health at a glance.
- **The Board Dashboard:** A governance-focused view of financial health, budget vs. actual, and milestone tracking.

### Data Cleanup & Governance
A dashboard is only as reliable as the data feeding it. We remediate the "data quality debt" that accumulates over years of staff transitions and growth.

**What we deliver:**
- **Systematic Deduplication:** Merging duplicate household and individual records while preserving full history.
- **Inactive Record Archiving:** Cleaning the database using church-specific criteria (last giving/attendance date) without losing historical data.
- **Field Standardization:** Normalizing name, address, and phone formats for reliable communication segmentation.
- **Ongoing Governance Policy:** Documented data entry standards and offboarding protocols that ensure your database stays clean.
