With an average of 5–8 disconnected tools, your staff spends more time on manual data entry than on ministry. Church Systems Integration connects your ChMS, giving platform, communication tools, and website into one coherent system — so attendance, donations, and communication data flows where it needs to go without re-keying.

This service also includes church website design and development. Your website is the front door of your ministry — where first-time visitors decide whether to visit, where members go for information, and where giving and event registration happen. A church website that integrates with your other tools ensures every system works together.

## What You Get [.h2 .font-bold .font-secondary]

- Integration plan for all your ministry tools (ChMS, giving, communication, website)
- Website design and development that connects to your existing systems
- Data flow mapping so information moves automatically between tools
- Elimination of duplicate data entry and conflicting records
- Ongoing support to keep your integrated systems running smoothly

## Why This Matters

When your tools don't talk to each other, your staff pays the price — hours of manual entry, duplicated records, and reports that never match. Church Systems Integration eliminates the disconnected-tool problem so your staff can focus on people, not paperwork. 80% of first-time visitors check your website before attending in person (Epic Life Creative) — an integrated site ensures they find current, accurate information.
