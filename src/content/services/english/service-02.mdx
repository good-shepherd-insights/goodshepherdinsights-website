Choosing a church management system shouldn't feel overwhelming. ChMS Selection & Management guides you through selection, implementation, and stewardship of your church management software — so your staff gets the full value from what you invest in, instead of paying for features that go unused.

With over 30 ChMS platforms on the market and average utilization under 40%, most churches are paying for tools their staff barely uses. The problem isn't the software — it's that no one helped you choose the right one, configure it for your ministry, and train your staff to use it well.

## What You Get [.h2 .font-bold .font-secondary]

- Objective evaluation of ChMS platforms based on your church's specific needs
- A structured selection process that removes vendor bias from the decision
- Implementation guidance so your new system works from day one
- Staff training recommendations to drive adoption and utilization
- Ongoing review to ensure your ChMS keeps serving your ministry as you grow

## Why This Matters

Churches often choose ChMS platforms based on a sales demo or a friend's recommendation — then discover the tool doesn't fit their ministry processes. ChMS Selection & Management puts a knowledgeable, vendor-neutral partner in your corner so you choose software that fits your church, not software that fits a sales quota.
